Technology Should You Buy a .co.ke or .com Domain Name if You Are in Kenya?

By John Gitonga, Published on January 12th, 2025

Introduction

If you're considering building a website in Kenya, one of the first questions that may come to mind is, "How much will it cost?" The answer depends on several factors, including the type of website you want, its features, and the expertise of the developer or agency you choose. In this blog, we’ll break down the costs associated with building a website in Kenya and what you can expect at each stage.

What is a Domain Name?

A domain name is your website’s unique address on the internet. Think of it as your digital home. The extension, like .co.ke or .com, is called a top-level domain (TLD).

• .com: A global domain used worldwide.

• .co.ke: A country-specific domain for Kenya.

.

1. Target Audience

• .co.ke: Ideal if your website primarily serves a Kenyan audience. It shows that your business is local, which can build trust among Kenyan users.

• .com: Perfect for businesses targeting an international audience or aiming for a global presence.

2. SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

• .co.ke: Can improve local SEO, helping your website rank higher in searches within Kenya.

• .com: Offers global SEO advantages, making it easier to rank in searches worldwide.

3. Availability

• .co.ke: Often has more available options, as it’s less commonly used than .com. You’re more likely to get your desired domain name.

• .com: Highly popular, which means many domain names may already be taken.

4. Cost

• .co.ke: Generally more affordable in Kenya.

• .com: Slightly more expensive, especially when purchased from international domain registrars.

5. Trust and Credibility

• .co.ke: Builds trust with Kenyan users, as it shows your commitment to the local market.

• .com: Universally recognized and trusted, making it suitable for a broader audience.

When to Choose .co.ke

• You are targeting Kenyan customers or clients.

• Your business is based in Kenya and focuses on local services.

• You want a domain name that reflects your Kenyan identity.

When to Choose .com

• Your audience extends beyond Kenya or you plan to expand globally.

• You want a domain name with universal appeal.

• You can’t find your preferred domain name under .co.ke.

Why Not Both?

For businesses with a local presence but global ambitions, buying both .co.ke and .com can be a smart move. This prevents others from using your brand name with a different extension and allows you to target both local and international audiences effectively.

How to Buy a Domain in Kenya

1. Choose a Domain Registrar

Look for trusted domain providers like Webhost Experts Kenya, Truehost, or international companies like GoDaddy and Namecheap.

2. Search for Your Domain Name

Use the registrar's search tool to check if your desired domain is available.

3. Register and Pay

Complete the purchase, and don’t forget to renew your domain annually to keep it active. It is important to note that you will need to buy a hosting package alongside the domain name if you do not already have hosting so that your domain can be found online as the link to a website.

Final Thoughts

The choice between a .co.ke and a .com domain depends on your target audience and goals. If your focus is on Kenya, a .co.ke domain is perfect. For international ambitions, .com is the way to go.
